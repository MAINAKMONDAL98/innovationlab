GLOBAL HAWK v2.0

21 Dec 2018 Soham Biswas

The GLOBAL HAWK v2.0 is a better and upgraded version of the previous v1.0 version. Additional features such as videography, voice control and navigation are added. Its fully autonomous with features like Stabilize, Alt Hold, Loiter, RTL (Return-to-Launch), Auto Tuning, Brake, Circle, Drift, Guided, Land, Position Hold, Sport and Follow Me. GESTURE CONTROLLED DRONE

21 Dec 2018 Subham Kundu, Souvik Som, Suman Basu , Soham Ghoshs

Now-a-days whenever we hear that a drone is made people make a perception that they have purchased all the items and assembled all those parts. There is no hard task in it as compared to other beautiful projects. So our main aim is to change this perception and for that we have designed a drone which is almost hand-made. Apart from the motors , Esc and battery everything we have designed or implemented in our own way. The flight controller, the transmitter and receiver and even the chassis we have designed in our own way. So this implementation makes our drone much more cost effective and one of the striking features is that it is gesture controlled unlike the usual remote controlled.

MAZE-DUINO

21 Dec 2018 Sumalyo Datta, Sayanti Dutta , Nilanjana Bandyopadhyay , Susmita Basu, Prateek Majumder , Souhardya Moitra

This project aims to build an embedded low-cost intelligent motion control and analytics system with improved stability and precision control using the Inertial Measurement Unit Sensors(eg Accelerometer) of any smartphone and Bluetooth connection to replicate exact orientation data in the model, creating, as a proof-of-concept, a hand gesture controlled maze with tilt sense, using a sophisticated network of servo motors and most importantly an Arduino UNO at its core. The orientation of the maze board is controlled by the smartphone, which can be used to play the game and control the ball on the board simply by moving the phone in the desired direction to steer the ball effectively, past the obstacles. The project has both software and hardware feeder projects with an ingeniously developed android app that codes for receiving sensor data and custom-made chassis for effective control and utilization of sensor data. Also, it has an attractive interface to play with therefore demonstrating the concept in a way that is both fun and easy to understand and interesting to experience.
