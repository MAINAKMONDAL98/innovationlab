WELCOME TO INNOVATION LAB

creative and make a different

What is engineering ? It means to build or to design something to solve real life problems .It breaks our previous notion of engineering which was to study and mug up . Now as we all know that to build and design something we need innovation. This means to introduce something new and which comes from team work and out of box thinking. All these things are important for an engineer .These things are provided under one roof in the innovation lab . It encourages the students to work on different projects which solves real life problems. It helps students to grow as a person and as an engineer. By working on different projects there both practical and theoretical knowledge increases by a large amount .They also learn how to work in teams by working on projects. As innovation lab consists of students from different trades of engineering it also helps students to grow as community. Our esteemed and highly qualified teachers acts as mentors for projects who provide us right direction and point out mistakes which helps students to make a better product. Innovation lab is a great platform to start for the competition of the world outside our college.
